Chocolate Brownies

Rich and delicious Chocolate brownies perfect for those with sweet tooth

Ingredients

  • 370g Condensed milk
  • 24g cacao powder
  • 350g dark chocolate
  • 4 large eggs
  • 80g flour
  • 150g butter
  • 250g milk chocolate
  • 60g walnuts chopped

Method:

Step 1

  1. Melt dark and milk chocolate with butter.
  2. Beat egg in mixer with Condensed milk.
  3. Then ad the melted chocolate with butter on top of egg and sugar and mix for 30 seconds.

Step 2

  1. Add flour, cacao powder to the mixture and keep mixing slowly to get soft dough dark colour.
  2. Add chopped walnuts.

Step 3

  1. In a baking tray bake for 40 minutes at 165 degrees.
  2. Cool completely in pan then cut into square.
  3. Enjoy your delicious home made brownies
